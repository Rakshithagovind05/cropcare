About the CropCare Project

CropCare is an innovative agriculture assistance platform designed to help farmers detect crop diseases instantly using image-based analysis. By uploading an image of an affected crop, the system identifies the type of disease and provides relevant solutions and preventive measures to restore plant health. The platform also offers insights into market trends, enabling farmers to make smart decisions about cultivation and sales.
